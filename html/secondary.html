The NC Revelers Orchestra, is a show and dance band in the tradition of the great touring bands of Glenn Miller, Count Basie, Les Brown and others. Our musicians are some of North Carolina's finest and our mission is to bring the best of the great american songbook of popular show and dance tunes to new audiences. For more information contact Henry Mason at 919 847 2263. For about the same cost of some much smaller bands the NC Revelers Orchestra can make your event one to be remembered for a lifetime and for all the right reasons.
